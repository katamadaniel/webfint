Who are we?

We are a tech start-up company offering solitions to small and medium scale Entrepreneurs in need of advertising, branding, design and consultation services.
We pride ourselves in having the best team with skills to much the industry standards in quality.
Our packages and offers are tailored to fit our clients needs by giving you a combination of all the services you need to make your business thrive.
We offer all this at a well structured price that is broken down to fit your budget.

Our mission is and will always be to put all business on the global map and give them a competitive edge in the market.
This will be made possible by our catalogue of services in Web development, Graphic design and advertising which we will enable your business become more visible to customers around you as well as online market place.
We also have a strong passion for building which includes building your business brand to international standards so that you can stand out among a crowd of businesses in your market.

Webfinit designs was was founded in Late 2021 by Mr.Daniel Katama a Computer Science graduate while working as an online Freelance developer and Entrepreneur.
The company was registered in mid 2022 after months of market research and refining the idea into a business that would operate in confinement to the laws of Kenya. The conception of this company was as a result of realizing the effects of Covid-19 on local businesses as well as the challange of sustaining new startups within the country.
The aim of the company was to help startups, as well as existing entreprises to create a digital presence and image through branding, advertising and and implementing online platforms for online orders, sales and interaction with clients.
